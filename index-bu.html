Collect Plastic
Collect Coins.

We like to reward you for your good deeds. With the Plastic Hero Project, you can earn PTH mainnet coins for saving the planet.

  • Mine unlimited PTH mainnet coins.
  • Earn one mainnet coin for every plastic bottle you deposit in our kiosk.
  • These mainnet coins can be exchanged for points (1 PTH: 10 points) or bridged for PTH-BEP20 tokens (1 PTH: 1 PTH-BEP20). However, it is not possible to convert points back into mainnet coins.

The Way To Your Rewards.

Simple. Drop plastic bottles into our kiosk via the application and earn your rewards easily.

  • Complete your registration using a QR code to become a member.
  • Deposit the plastic bottles into the kiosk. We recycle them and reward you with PTH mainnet coins.
  • The collected plastic bottles will be processed and stored.
